‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is MindWell a replacement for therapy?

No, MindWell is designed to complement, not replace, professional mental health care. We use evidence-based techniques but cannot provide clinical diagnosis or treatment. For serious mental health concerns, please consult with a licensed therapist or physician.

How secure is my personal information?

MindWell is HIPAA-compliant and uses military-grade encryption (AES-256) to protect your data. Your therapy sessions are encrypted both in transit and at rest. We never share your personal health information with advertisers or third parties.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription anytime through your device's app store settings (iOS App Store or Google Play Store). Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

What happens to my data if I delete my account?

When you request account deletion, all of your personal health information is permanently deleted within 30 days. This includes therapy sessions, mood data, and personal preferences. This action cannot be undone.

Does MindWell work offline?

MindWell requires an internet connection for AI therapy sessions. However, some features like crisis resources, breathing exercises, and emergency contacts are available offline for your safety.

How does the AI therapist work?

Our AI uses advanced natural language processing to provide therapeutic conversations based on Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) principles. While sophisticated, it's important to remember that AI responses are generated by algorithms, not human therapists.

Is there a limit to therapy sessions?

Premium subscribers have unlimited access to therapy sessions. Free trial users can access up to 3 full sessions during the 7-day trial period.

What if the app detects I'm in crisis?

MindWell has built-in crisis detection algorithms. If concerning language is detected, the app will immediately provide crisis resources and emergency contact information. In severe cases, we may recommend you contact emergency services immediately.

üîß Technical Support

Common Issues & Solutions

App won't load or crashes

  • Restart the app completely
  • Check your internet connection
  • Update to the latest app version
  • Restart your device
  • Contact support if issues persist

Voice features not working

  • Check microphone permissions in device settings
  • Ensure you're in a quiet environment
  • Test your device's microphone with other apps
  • Try using text-based conversations instead

Biometric authentication issues

  • Verify Face ID/Touch ID is enabled for MindWell
  • Clean your device's sensors
  • Re-enroll your biometrics in device settings
  • Use passcode as backup authentication
