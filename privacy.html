MindWell Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 15, 2025

HIPAA-Compliant Mental Health App
MindWell is designed with privacy-first principles and complies with HIPAA regulations to protect your mental health information.

1. Information We Collect

Protected Health Information (PHI)

  • Therapy session conversations and transcripts
  • Mental health assessments (PHQ-9 scores)
  • Mood tracking data and progress metrics
  • Crisis intervention records
  • Therapeutic goals and outcomes

Personal Information

  • Email address (for account recovery only)
  • Device identifiers (for security purposes)
  • App usage analytics (anonymized)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Therapeutic Services: Provide personalized AI therapy sessions
  • Safety Monitoring: Crisis intervention and risk assessment
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor your mental health journey
  • App Improvement: Enhance features through anonymized usage data

3. Data Security & Encryption

Military-Grade Security:
  • AES-256-GCM encryption for all data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for data transmission
  • Certificate pinning for API communications
  • Biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID)
  • iOS Keychain and Android Keystore integration

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We DO NOT sell, rent, or share your personal health information.

Limited Third-Party Services (HIPAA Business Associates)

  • Google Gemini AI: Processes therapy conversations (BAA in place)
  • ElevenLabs: Voice synthesis for audio responses (BAA required)
  • Firebase: Authentication services only (no PHI stored)

5. Your Privacy Rights

Under HIPAA, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your health information
  • Amendment: Request corrections to your health information
  • Restriction: Request limits on use/disclosure of your information
  • Accounting: Request a list of disclosures
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all data

Data Retention

  • Therapy session data: Retained while account is active
  • Account deletion: All PHI permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Crisis records: Retained for 7 years per HIPAA requirements
  • Audit logs: Retained for 6 years for compliance

6. Crisis Intervention

In cases of imminent danger or self-harm risk, we may disclose information to:

  • Emergency services (911)
  • Mental health crisis teams
  • Designated emergency contacts (with your consent)

7. Children's Privacy

MindWell is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors without parental consent.

8. International Users

MindWell is designed for US users and complies with US privacy laws including HIPAA. International users should review their local privacy regulations.

9. Data Breaches

In the unlikely event of a data breach involving PHI, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 60 days
  • Report to HHS within 60 days (as required by HIPAA)
  • Implement immediate containment measures
  • Provide free credit monitoring if applicable

10. Cookies and Tracking

We do NOT use tracking cookies or advertising pixels. Essential cookies are used only for:

  • Authentication and session management
  • App functionality and user preferences
  • Security and fraud prevention

11. Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of material changes via:

  • In-app notification
  • Email notification (if provided)
  • Updated policy with effective date

12. Contact Information

Privacy Officer: privacy@mindwell-app.com

HIPAA Compliance: hipaa@mindwell-app.com

General Support: support@mindwell-app.com

Data Deletion Requests: delete@mindwell-app.com

13. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For international users, our legal basis for processing includes:

  • Consent: You voluntarily provide health information
  • Vital Interests: Crisis intervention and safety measures
  • Legitimate Interest: App functionality and improvement
